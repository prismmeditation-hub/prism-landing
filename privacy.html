01
Who We Are
PRISM is a meditation and journaling app created by Eddie Mac ([email protected]). References to "we," "us," or "our" refer to the PRISM app and its operator.
02
What Data We Collect
We collect only what is necessary to provide the app experience:
  • Your first name — entered during onboarding
  • Voice recordings — recorded during your daily practice sessions and intention setting
  • Transcripts — text generated from your voice recordings
  • App usage data — which days you have completed, your settings preferences, and your commitment tracking
We do not collect your email address, location, payment information, or any data that identifies you beyond your first name.
03
How We Use Your Data
Your data is used solely to provide and improve your personal PRISM experience:
  • To generate personalized AI insights from your session transcripts
  • To track your progress through the 5-day challenge
  • To display your journey summary and commitment history
  • To pre-fill your name on future uses of the app
04
Voice Recordings and AI Processing
Your voice recordings are transcribed locally on your device where possible. Transcripts are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate your personal insights. This is the only time your data leaves your device.

Anthropic processes this data solely to generate your response. API inputs and outputs are automatically deleted from Anthropic's servers after 7 days and are never used for model training. You can review Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.
05
Data Storage
All your recordings, transcripts, and progress data are stored locally on your device using your phone's secure storage. We do not operate servers or databases that store your personal data.
06
Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:
  • Anthropic — AI insight generation. Privacy policy
  • RevenueCat — subscription management (paid features only). Privacy policy
  • Apple — app delivery and device infrastructure. Privacy policy
07
Your Rights (UK and EU Users)
If you are located in the UK or European Union, you have the following rights under UK GDPR and EU GDPR:
  • Right of access — you may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — you may request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — you may request deletion of your data
  • Right to withdraw consent — you may withdraw consent at any time by deleting the app, which removes all locally stored data from your device
  • Right to lodge a complaint — UK users may contact the Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk). EU users may contact their local data protection authority.
To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [email protected]. We will respond within 30 days.
08
Children's Privacy
PRISM is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
09
Data Retention
Your data is stored on your device for as long as you use the app. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data. Anthropic automatically deletes any transmitted transcript data within 7 days.
10
Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
